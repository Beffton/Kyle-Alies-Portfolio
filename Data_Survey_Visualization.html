Data Survey Visualization

After cleaning the survey data from this project, I decided to visualize the data in both Microsoft PowerBi and Tableau with different styles. The visualization on the left was done in PowerBi, and the visualization on the right was done in Tableau.

Some useful information:

  • Data Scientists are the highest overall earners in the data industry
  • Python seems to be the favourite programming language
  • Most people in this survey did not start out in the data industry
  • Salary seems to be aspect of data that people are the least satisfied with

One of the challenges I faced was needing to go back to my python file multiple times to clean up pieces of data that slipped though the initial sweep, for the next time I should do more through checks of the data before exporting to a CSV. Another challenge that I faced with this project was that I was not able to publicize my PowerBi copy of this dashboard due to not owning a PowerBi Pro license, as a solution, I decided to reimagine this visualization in Tableau trying to highlight most of the same points, while also bringing up some new ones like how many people swapped into data.
